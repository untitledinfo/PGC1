🚀 PGC Host Is Now Live — Rent Your Own Minecraft Server!

The Pakistan Gamers Community (PGC) is proud to launch PGC Host — our very own Minecraft server hosting service. Whether you want to run a private SMP with friends or build a whole new community, you can now rent your own server through PGC.

🖥️ What Is PGC Host?

PGC Host lets you spin up your own Minecraft server, backed by the same team that keeps Warrior Lifesteal SMP fast and reliable. No technical experience needed — pick a plan, and your server is ready to play.

✅ Why Choose PGC Host

  • ⚡ Fast performance: Low-latency hosting across Pakistan
  • 🛡️ DDoS protection: Enterprise-grade security included
  • 🎮 Java & Bedrock support: Full crossplay compatibility
  • 🧑‍💻 PGC support team: Real help from people who play

📦 Get Started

Ready to launch your own server? Head over to web.pgcmc.fun to view plans and get your server running today.
